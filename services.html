Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. Simple logo designs typically take 5-7 days, while comprehensive branding projects may take 2-4 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes! All our packages include revisions. The number of revisions depends on your chosen package, with our Professional package offering unlimited revisions to ensure your complete satisfaction.

What file formats do you provide?

We provide files in all commonly used formats including PNG, JPG, PDF, AI, EPS, and SVG. Our Professional and Enterprise packages include vector files for maximum scalability and print quality.

Can you help with supply chain management?

Absolutely! Our supply services team specializes in end-to-end supply chain management, from procurement and inventory management to logistics and distribution. We tailor our services to your specific industry needs.

Do you work with businesses of all sizes?

Yes, we work with everyone from startups and small businesses to large enterprises. Our flexible packages and custom solutions ensure we can meet the needs of any organization, regardless of size or budget.
