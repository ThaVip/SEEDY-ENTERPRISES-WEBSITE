About Us

SEEDY ENTERPRISES (NIGERIA) LIMITED is a wholly indigenous dynamic and fully integrated printing outfit with special emphasis on the total quality concept and proactive service delivery. We are one of the leading technologically advanced printing outfits in Abuja. SEEDY ENTERPRISES (NIGERIA) LIMITED was incorporated on 21st of April, 1995 to, among others, engage in the business of printing, publishing and general trading. We commenced operations in 1996 at our head office, in Abuja with 5 technical and 7 administrative staff with customers spread all over the 36 states of Nigeria and Abuja (FCT). Within the first year of our operations, we were among the three best printing and publishing houses in Abuja.
